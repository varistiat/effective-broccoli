If I could pin one feeling to this page, it would be the comfort of finding someone who makes ordinary moments feel rare.

I do not want this to feel like clicking through slides. I want each part of us to have its own room to breathe, like turning to the next page of something handmade.

So this page stays quiet on purpose — soft paper, a small note, and enough space for the feeling to land.
